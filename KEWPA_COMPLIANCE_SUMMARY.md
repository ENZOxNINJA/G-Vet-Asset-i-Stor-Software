# KEW.PA Asset Management System - Complete Compliance Implementation

## Overview
This system implements comprehensive Malaysian government asset management compliance following KEW.PA (Kewangan Pendaftaran Aset) and KEW.PS (Kewangan Pengurusan Stor) frameworks for public sector organizations.

## Implemented KEW.PA Forms

### Asset Reception and Registration
- **KEW.PA-1 (Asset Reception)**: Complete asset receiving process with supplier verification
- **KEW.PA-2 (Asset Rejection)**: Formal rejection documentation for non-compliant assets
- **KEW.PA-3 (Asset Registration)**: Comprehensive asset registration with all required government fields

### Asset Inspection and Verification
- **KEW.PA-11 (Inspection Assignment)**: Inspector appointment and inspection scheduling
- **KEW.PA-12 (Inspection Report)**: Detailed inspection findings and asset condition reporting
- **KEW.PA-13 (Inspection Certificate)**: Official certification of completed inspections

- **KEW.PA-15 (Low-Value Asset Maintenance)**: Maintenance records for assets <RM2,000
- **KEW.PA-16 (Annual Maintenance Report)**: Comprehensive yearly maintenance summary

### Asset Movement and Transfer
- **KEW.PA-9 (Asset Movement)**: Complete asset movement tracking and authorization
- **KEW.PA-17 (Asset Transfer Request)**: Formal transfer request and approval process
- **KEW.PA-18 (Asset Transfer Record)**: Official transfer documentation and handover

### Asset Disposal Management
- **KEW.PA-19 (Technical Assessment - PEP)**: Professional technical evaluation for disposal
- **KEW.PA-20 (Disposal Board Formation)**: Disposal committee appointment and evaluation
- **KEW.PA-21 (Disposal Recommendation)**: Board recommendations and disposal methods
- **KEW.PA-22 (Disposal Execution)**: Actual disposal process and witnessing
- **KEW.PA-23 (Disposal Verification)**: Final verification and certification

### Additional Forms
- **KEW.PA-10 (Damage Report)**: Asset damage documentation and assessment
- **KEW.PA-4 to KEW.PA-8**: Loan request, approval, and return processes
